Fxteeyma

In a world where fashion often leans towards fleeting trends, Fxteeyma stands as a symbol of empowerment and creative self-expression for Muslim women. My mission is deeply rooted in the desire to make
abayas accessible to all Muslim women, regardless of their sewing experience or financial constraints.

My mission is to teach Muslim women how to sew their own abayas, especially those who are complete beginners or cannot afford the soaring prices of modest clothing. I'm dedicated to making the art of abaya-making
accessible to all, while honoring the rich traditions of Islamic modesty and identity.

Why Sew Your Own Abaya?
Sewing your own abaya is a profound act of empowerment and devotion. It's about:
  1. Affordability: In a world where modest clothing can be expensive, sewing your abaya is a cost-effective solution that doesn't compromise your faith.

  2. Skill Empowerment: I cater to complete beginners, providing step-by-step lessons that empower you to master the art of abaya-making, ensuring that you can create clothing that is in line with our beliefs.

  3. Customisation: Your abaya is more than just clothing; it's an expression of your faith, style, and cultural identity. We guide you in creating a piece that's a true reflection of your values.

  4. Sustainability: By sewing your own abaya, you embrace a more sustainable approach to fashion, aligning with the values of mindfulness and simplicity cherished by many Muslims.

  5. Offerings: Fxteeyma is more than just a sewing resource; it's your creative hub for abaya making, deeply rooted in Islamic tradition. Here's what I offer:

    • Abaya Patterns: I provide a range of sewing paper patterns designed for complete beginners. These patterns are your blueprint to creating simple yet elegant abayas.

    • One-on-One Lessons for complete beginners My personalised one-on-one lessons ensure that you receive expert guidance, tailored to your skill level and your desire to embrace Islamic modesty.

    • Handmade Abayas: While I'm all about teaching you to sew your abaya, I do also occasionally offer handmade abayas.
